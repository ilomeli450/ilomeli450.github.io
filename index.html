About

I'm a Full Stack Developer and Tech Lead at FedEx, where I've grown from FSW I to FSW III since 2021 — building and owning backend systems that station couriers across the country depend on every day.

My core work is a Spring Boot application on Pivotal Cloud Foundry (PCF) that manages hot and cold space storage retrieval for courier operations. The platform produces critical JSON API responses and PDF manifests — including fully DOT-compliant hazmat manifests required for the safe and legal handling of regulated shipments.

As tech lead I carry cross-functional responsibility: managing an overseas development team to accelerate delivery while coordinating the platform's international rollout as FedEx expands the system globally.

Outside work I build community-focused tools — a bilingual youth soccer club platform for Escuela de Futbol GDL-Razo in Costa Mesa, CA, and a live currency rate web app used by a local Santa Ana barbershop and its customers daily.
